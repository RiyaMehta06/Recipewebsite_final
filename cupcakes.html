Eggless cupcakes

3/4 cup vegetable oil
1 1/4 cup sugar
1 cup yogurt
1 1/2 cup maida
3/4 cup cocoa powder
Pinch of salt
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 cup milk

Procedure

Preheat the oven at 180 degrees C
In a mixing bowl, add oil and sugar. Mix.
In the same bowl, add yogurt,milk and mix
Place a sieve over the bowl, sift flour, cocoa powder, salt, baking powder and soda
Once sifted, mix all ingredients well
Use an ice cream scoop to fill 3/4 of each cupcale mold
Keep in oven for 20-25 minutes. Your cupcakes are ready!
