Eggless croissants

Ingredients

2 cups refined flour
1/4 cup sugar
1 1/4 tsp salt
2 tbsp milk powder
1 cup warm milk
1.5 tsp instant dry yeast
75 grams of butter

Procedure

add warm milk, sugar and instant yeast to a bowl. Keep aside for ten minutes
In a mixing bowl, add refined flour, salt, milk powder . Add the yeast mixture and knead the dough
Cover the dough with cling wrap and leave for two hours. Dough should be double the size
Once the dough is ready, make equally sized balls
Roll each balls into circles
Layer all the circles on one plate. Put butter between each roll
Cover this with sling wrap and refrigerate for two hours
After two hours, use a pizza cutter to cut into triangular pieces
Take one triangle piece and make a small slit in the edge. Stretch it, stuff chocolate and roll in an upwards direction
Grease a baking tray and place the croissants, maintain a gap
After thirty minutes, grease with milk and preheat the oven to 180 degrees
Keep in oven for 20-25 minutes. Your corissants are ready!
