Eggless donuts

Ingredients

2 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
1/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup milk
3 tablespoons butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Procedure

warm 1/2 cup milk
Add 1/4 cup sugar and 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast. Mix well!
Add 1 cup whole flour
Mix well. Cover with a lid for 20-30 mins
Add remaining one cup of flour, 3 tablespoons butter, 1 tsp vanilla extract
Mix and knead the dough. Add one cup of milk
Cover the dough and leave for 45 mins to 1 hour
Line a tray with parchment paper
Knead the dough . Sprinkle some flour and roll all of it together into a circle
Keep and press the donut cutter on the dough. Do this multiple times.
After thirty minutes, grease with milk and preheat the oven to 180 degrees
Preheat the oven at 180 degrees celsius
Bake for 20-25 minutes. Donuts are ready!
